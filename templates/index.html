Welcome to the Cyberstudies World!

Exploring the human, social, and technical aspects of cybersecurity.

Here we are doing research on a bunch of really interesting topics related to cyber security. Looking at cybersecurity from a different perspective, we are trying to understand the human and social aspects of cyber security. We are also looking at the technical aspects of cyber security.

Research shouldn't just end with the publication of a paper. We are trying to make our research more accessible and usable for everyone, and more interactive and engaging through the use of visualizations of our data, results, and findings via web visualization and interactive tools.

Publishing just raw CVE files and scripts to run is not user friendly, that's why we are creating a web interface to interact with our data and results, making it more accessible and usable for everyone.
